About Cybersecurity Advancement Program (C.A.P.)

The Cybersecurity Advancement Program (C.A.P.) is a bold, youth-led initiative aligned with Rwanda’s National Cybersecurity Strategy (2024–2029). More than a training program, C.A.P. is a movement uniting young Rwandans, educational institutions, industry leaders, and government to protect and advance Rwanda’s digital future with integrity, honor, and innovation.

As Rwanda accelerates its digital transformation, cybersecurity becomes critical to national safety, economic growth, and sovereignty. C.A.P. bridges the “Civilization Gap” by equipping a new generation of ethical, skilled cybersecurity professionals ready to defend, innovate, and lead in Africa’s digital landscape.

Vision & Mission

Vision:
To establish Rwanda as a regional powerhouse in cybersecurity by empowering youth to become ethical, skilled, and impactful digital defenders.

Mission:
To advance cybersecurity education, foster innovation and leadership, promote digital ethics, and build a resilient, secure digital ecosystem through youth engagement.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and strong moral principles
  • Honor: Upholding respect and dignity in all endeavors
  • Responsibility: Committing to protect and serve the digital community
  • Innovation: Driving creative solutions for evolving cyber challenges
  • Accountability: Ensuring transparency and ethical conduct
  • National Service: Serving Rwanda’s digital security and prosperity

Program Pillars

Education & Skills Development
Hands-on training in ethical hacking, digital forensics, cyber law, and leadership, coupled with industry-recognized certifications.

Innovation & Leadership
Cyber hackathons, solution labs, and leadership bootcamps that inspire creativity and nurture future tech leaders.

Advocacy & Awareness
Campaigns and youth ambassador programs that raise public cyber awareness and promote responsible digital behavior.

Career & Entrepreneurship
Internship placements, startup incubation, and a thriving alumni network supporting career growth and innovation.

Integrity & Accountability Framework

Participants earn a unique Digital Ethics ID linking their training, certifications, and commitment to ethical conduct. Graduates undergo annual integrity check-ins, receive peer evaluations, and are supported by a strict violation protocol ensuring only responsible professionals remain in Rwanda’s Cyber Talent Registry—a trusted national resource.

Why C.A.P. Builds Defenders, Not Hackers

C.A.P. teaches cybersecurity as a shield, not a weapon. Our students learn to prevent cyberattacks ethically and legally, taking a solemn Cyber Ethics Oath. Mentored by experienced professionals, they become analysts, digital forensic experts, and ethical hackers committed to defending Rwanda’s digital space. Avoiding cybersecurity education increases vulnerability—C.A.P. offers a proactive, principled path forward.

Our Goals (2025–2027)

  • Train 1,000+ youth in cybersecurity skills
  • Achieve 70% job or entrepreneurship placement for graduates
  • Support 30+ institutions with alumni-led cybersecurity services
  • Influence 10+ cybersecurity policies through youth advocacy
  • Establish 50+ active cybersecurity clubs nationwide

Sustainability & Growth

C.A.P. sustains impact through paid advanced certifications, alumni-led service teams, partnerships with tech companies and donors, and a membership model that fosters ongoing community support.

Join the Movement

Rwanda’s digital future depends on strong defenders. C.A.P. calls on:

  • Government: Integrate our graduates and co-create cybersecurity standards
  • Private Sector: Hire and empower certified cybersecurity professionals
  • Schools & Communities: Lead cybersecurity awareness and education
  • Investors & Donors: Invest in Africa’s ethical and innovative cyber future
